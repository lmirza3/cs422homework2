Instructions

Simply drag the white circle to turn on the unit.

Once you have successfully turned on the device, you will be redirected to a screen that contains the home page, buttons representing common preset options, and a menu tab that can be clicked to open for cooking options beyond the commonly used presets.

If you have chosen the preset option, then you simply have to choose the desired circle indicated your desired darkness on your toast or bagel. You won't need to set the temperature or mode if you choose a preset; the toaster will automatically choose the temperature and time that works best for your desired food and darkness level.

The presets are also a good way to organize presets that are used more often. This allows for a quicker navigational system for commonly used functions and puts away the more intricate features unless they are truly needed. The presence of solid white icons against a darkened background offer a consistent, easy to read, elegant user interface that is functional and aesthetically pleasing.

If you have chosen to utilize the menu option, you will be directed to another screen that prompts you to choose your desired cooking mode. There are three cooking modes which include bake, broil, and toast.

Once you have chosen a cooking mode, you will be directed to a screen with a number pad that allows you to pad in your desired temperature level. Tap the checkmark in the bottom right hand corner to continue.

Once you have chosen a temperature, you will be redirected to a screen with another number pad that will allow you to choose your desired cooking time. Again, tap the bottom right hand corner to continue

Once you have entered your desired temperature, cooking time, and cook mode, the toaster will turn on, displaying the information that you have entered and the remaining cook time, along with a cancel button along the bottom of the screen.
