Toaster Oven Solution

To use, simply swipe the black circle to open

This will allow you to turn on the device.

Once you have opened the device, you will be redirected to a screen that contains the home page, a preheat button, slides to the common preset options, and a menu tab that can be clicked to open.
If you have chosen the preset option, then you simply have to swipe left or right in order to choose your desired darkness on your toast or bagel.
If you have chosen to use the menu, another menu will open up prompting you to choose your desired cooking mode. There are three cooking modes which include bake, broil, and toast.
Once you have chosen a cooking mode, you will be directed to a screen with a number pad that allows you to pad in your desired temperature level.
Once you have chosen a temperature, you will be redirected to a screen with another number pad that will allow you to choose your desired cooking time.
In order to continue, you have to press the check mark in the bottom right corner.
Once you have entered your desired temperature, cooking time, and cook mode, the toaster will turn on, displaying the information that you have entered and the remaining cook time, along with a cancel button along the bottom of the screen.
